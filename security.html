Security at Black Mesa

At Black Mesa, we prioritize the safety and security of our personnel and research. As a government property-owned facility, we employ the highest standards of security measures.

Our security includes:

  • Guards: Trained personnel who monitor and maintain the safety of the facility.
  • HECU Marines: Highly trained elite soldiers providing advanced protection and response capabilities.

With these measures in place, you can be assured of a secure environment for groundbreaking research and technology.
